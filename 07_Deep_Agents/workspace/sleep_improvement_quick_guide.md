### Your Specific Issues & Solutions

#### Problem: Inconsistent Bedtime (10pm-1am)
**Solution: Gradual Shift Method**
- Week 1: Focus only on consistent wake time
- Week 2+: Move bedtime 30-45 minutes earlier every 2-3 days
- Timeline: 10-14 days to go from 1am to 10pm bedtime

#### Problem: Phone Usage in Bed  
**Solution: Digital Boundaries**
- Immediate: Phone stays outside bedroom
- Replace with: Physical book, journaling, meditation
- Use blue light glasses if screens needed in evening

#### Problem: Morning Fatigue Despite Sleep
**Solution: Sleep Quality Enhancement**
- Bright light exposure within 30 minutes of waking
- Consistent sleep schedule (most important factor)
- Cool bedroom temperature
- No snooze button (fragments sleep)

### Evening (2-3 hours before bed)
- Dim lights, avoid blue light
- Light dinner, avoid large meals
- Begin wind-down routine

### 1 Hour Before Bed
- No screens (phone, TV, computer)
- Pre-sleep routine activities only
- Keep bedroom cool and dark

### Morning (First 30 minutes)
- Get up at consistent time (no snooze)
- Bright light exposure immediately
- Hydrate and move body
- Protein-rich breakfast within 1 hour
