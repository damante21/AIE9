# Alex's 2-Week Vegetarian Meal Plan

## Overview
A comprehensive vegetarian meal plan designed to improve energy levels and support better sleep quality.

**Components:** Breakfast, lunch, dinner, and two snacks daily  
**Focus:** Energy-boosting foods and sleep-supportive nutrients

---

## Nutritional Guidelines

### Key Protein Sources
- Lentils, chickpeas, beans, quinoa, tofu, nuts, seeds, whole grains

### Energy-Boosting Foods
- Quinoa, millet, oats, fruits (bananas, apples), leafy greens, sweet potatoes, healthy fats (avocado, nuts)

### Sleep-Supportive Foods
- Almonds, chamomile, kiwis, cherries, dark chocolate, foods rich in magnesium and melatonin

### Anxiety Management Foods
- Berries, leafy greens, whole grains, avocados, dark chocolate

---

## Meal Prep Tips
1. **Batch Cooking:** Prepare larger quantities of grains and legumes to use throughout the week
2. **Frozen Goods:** Use frozen fruits and vegetables for convenience and nutrition
3. **Storage:** Use clear containers for easy visibility and access
4. **Time Management:** Dedicate a few hours one day per week for meal prepping

## Key Recipes

### Overnight Oats
**Ingredients:**
- 1/2 cup oats
- 1 tablespoon chia seeds
- 1 cup almond milk
- Handful of blueberries

**Instructions:**
In a jar, combine all ingredients. Refrigerate overnight. Enjoy cold in the morning.

### Quinoa Salad
**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup cooked quinoa
- 1 cup chickpeas (rinsed and drained)
- 1 diced cucumber
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 lemon (juiced)
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Season to taste. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

### Stuffed Bell Peppers
**Ingredients:**
- 4 bell peppers, tops cut and seeds removed
- 1 cup cooked brown rice
- 1 cup black beans
- 1/2 cup corn
- Spices (cumin, paprika, garlic powder)

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
2. Mix rice, beans, corn, and spices
3. Fill peppers with mixture
4. Bake for 25 minutes until peppers are tender
