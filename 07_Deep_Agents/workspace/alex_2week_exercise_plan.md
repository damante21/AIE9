# Alex's 2-Week Exercise Plan

## Overview
A comprehensive 2-week exercise routine designed to build consistency and improve energy levels. 30-minute morning workouts, 3 times per week.

## Weekly Schedule
- **Week 1 & 2:**
  - **Monday:** Workout 1/4
  - **Wednesday:** Workout 2/5
  - **Friday:** Workout 3/6

## Exercise Structure
Each workout includes:
- **Warm-Up:** 5 minutes
- **Main Workout:** 20 minutes
- **Cool-Down:** 5 minutes

---

## Warm-Up Routine (5 minutes for every session)
Perform each exercise for 30 seconds:
- Arm Circles
- Leg Swings
- Torso Twists
- High Knees

---

## Week 1 Workouts

### Workout 1 (Monday Week 1)
**Main Workout (20 minutes):**
- **Bodyweight Squats**: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  - Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, lower down as if sitting in a chair and then rise back up
- **Incline Push-Ups**: 3 sets of 5-10 reps
  - Place hands on an elevated surface, lower body towards hands, then push back up
- **Standing Calf Raises**: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  - Stand tall, rise up onto toes, then lower back down
**Cool Down (5 minutes):** Stretch major muscle groups (hold each stretch for 20-30 seconds)

### Workout 2 (Wednesday Week 1)
**Main Workout (20 minutes):**
- **Chair Step-Ups**: 3 sets of 10 reps each leg
  - Step onto a chair with one foot and bring the other foot up, then step back down
- **Side Lunges**: 3 sets of 8 reps each side
  - Step out to the side, bend the knee of the stepping leg while keeping the other leg straight
- **Wall Sit**: 2 sets of 20-30 seconds
  - Lean against the wall with knees bent at 90 degrees
**Cool Down (5 minutes):** Stretch major muscle groups

### Workout 3 (Friday Week 1)
**Main Workout (20 minutes):**
- **Knee Raises**: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  - Stand tall, raise knees alternately towards your chest
- **Plank** (modified on knees if needed): 2 sets of 20-30 seconds
  - Keep body straight from head to knees, hold the position
- **Glute Bridges**: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  - Lie on your back with knees bent, lift hips towards the ceiling
**Cool Down (5 minutes):** Stretch major muscle groups

---

## Week 2 Workouts

### Workout 4 (Monday Week 2)
**Main Workout (20 minutes):**
- **Side Leg Raises**: 3 sets of 10-15 reps each leg
- **Incline Push-Ups**: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
- **Standing Calf Raises**: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
**Cool Down (5 minutes):** Stretch major muscle groups

### Workout 5 (Wednesday Week 2)
**Main Workout (20 minutes):**
- **Chair Step-Ups**: 2 sets of 12 reps each leg
- **Plank**: 3 sets of 20-30 seconds
- **Bodyweight Squats**: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
**Cool Down (5 minutes):** Stretch major muscle groups

### Workout 6 (Friday Week 2)
**Main Workout (20 minutes):**
- **Side Lunges**: 3 sets of 10 reps each side
- **Glute Bridges**: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
- **Knee Raises**: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
**Cool Down (5 minutes):** Stretch major muscle groups

---

## Progression Tips
- Gradually increase the number of reps or sets each week, if comfortable
- Modify the exercises by adjusting the range of motion or the number of repetitions based on your fitness level

## Exercise Modifications
- Use a wall or chair for support with balance-related exercises
- Decrease the incline for push-ups if they're too difficult

## Motivation Tips
- Set small, achievable goals for each week
- Track your workouts to visualize progress
- Celebrate small victories and stay committed to your routine

This structured plan emphasizes consistency and energy improvement, fostering a sustainable exercise habit you can easily follow.
