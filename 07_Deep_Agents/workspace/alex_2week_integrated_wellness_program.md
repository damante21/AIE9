# Alex's 2-Week Integrated Wellness Program

## Program Overview
A comprehensive 2-week wellness program combining exercise, nutrition, and stress management/sleep optimization. Designed specifically for your goals of building consistent exercise habits, improving diet, and managing work stress while enhancing sleep quality.

---

## Daily Schedule Template

### Morning Routine (30-45 minutes)
1. **Mindfulness Practice** (10-15 minutes) - See daily variations in stress/sleep plan
2. **Exercise** (30 minutes) - Monday/Wednesday/Friday only
3. **Nutritious Breakfast** - Follow meal plan

### Workday Integration
- **Stress Management Techniques** - Throughout the day as specified in daily plan
- **Mindful Lunch** - Following meal plan
- **Afternoon Snack** - Energy-supporting options from meal plan

### Evening Routine (45-60 minutes)
1. **Dinner** - Following meal plan
2. **Evening Snack** (if needed)
3. **Wind-Down Activities** (30 minutes before bed)
4. **Sleep Optimization** - Consistent bedtime routine

## Integration Tips

### Making It Sustainable
- **Flexibility:** Adapt timing based on your schedule
- **Preparation:** Meal prep on weekends
- **Accountability:** Track daily in a journal or app
- **Self-Compassion:** Don't aim for perfection

### Customization Options
- **Exercise:** Modify intensity based on energy levels
- **Meals:** Swap similar ingredients based on preferences
- **Stress Management:** Focus more on techniques that resonate
- **Timing:** Adjust practice times to fit your schedule

---

## Emergency Protocols

### High Stress Days
- Prioritize breathing exercises
- Use the 5-minute meditation
- Choose easier meal options (pre-prepped items)
- Extend evening wind-down time

### Low Energy Days
- Modify exercise intensity (walk instead of full workout)
- Focus on protein-rich meals and snacks
- Practice gentle mindfulness
- Prioritize sleep optimization

### Time-Crunched Days
- Minimum viable routine: 5-minute breathing, healthy breakfast, 15-minute evening wind-down
- Use frozen/pre-prepped meal options
- Walking meeting for stress management
